Privacy Policy for Counter App

Last Updated: June 22, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Counter App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application. Counter App is a simple counting application designed for authorized users to count people and store count data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Account Information

For authorized app users, we collect and store:

  • Name (for user identification)
  • Phone number (for user identification and contact)
  • App access password (for authentication)

2.2 Usage Data

We collect:

  • Count data generated through the app (numerical counts)
  • Timestamps of when counts are submitted

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information about individuals being counted
  • Device location data
  • Photos, videos, or other media
  • Contacts or other personal data from your device
  • Browsing history or other app usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate and authorize app access
  • To store and manage counting data
  • To identify which user submitted specific count data
  • To provide technical support when needed

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Access to the app is restricted through password authentication, and only authorized users can access the application and its data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With our service providers (Google Firebase) who assist in app operation under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Firebase for data storage and authentication. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
